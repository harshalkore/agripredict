Process

How It Works

From raw mandi records to actionable sell-day advice — four steps, one decision.

01 / DATA
📊
Historical Price Collection
Monthly modal prices from Lasalgaon, Pune, and Latur APMCs — verified against agmarknet.gov.in, NHRDF, and Business Standard records from 2019 to 2025.
02 / TRAIN
🧠
BiLSTM Training
Bidirectional LSTM with 128 units, BatchNorm, and Huber loss reads daily price sequences both forward and backward — capturing seasonal spikes like the 2023 tomato crisis.
03 / ENSEMBLE
⚡
LSTM + ARIMA Ensemble
LSTM (70%) captures non-linear patterns. ARIMA (5,1,2) (30%) models linear trends and seasonality. Combined, they outperform either model alone.
04 / DECIDE
🌾
Farmer Recommendation
Market reality check, profit/loss calculation (CACP 2024-25 costs), MSP alert for Soybean, and plain-language action advice.
Technology Stack

Built for Real Accuracy

🔬
Deep Learning
Bidirectional LSTM (128 units) + BatchNormalization + Dropout (0.2). Trained with Huber loss for robustness against extreme price spikes. 60-day lookback window.

Verified Data Sources
Prices cross-verified from agmarknet.gov.in, NHRDF bulletins, Business Standard, CEIC/Directorate of Economics & Statistics, and KhetiVyapar.
